
# Task 3 – Customer Churn Prediction

Objective:
Identify bank customers who are likely to leave the bank.

Approach:

Cleaned and prepared the dataset, encoding categorical variables.
Explored data and trained a classification model to predict churn.
Analyzed feature importance to understand what influences churn.

Results & Insights:

Low balance, short tenure, and certain regions increase churn risk.
Feature importance showed which factors matter most for retention strategies.
Learned how to combine data processing, modeling, and insights for business decisions.
