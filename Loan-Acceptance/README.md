# Task 5 – Personal Loan Acceptance Prediction

Objective:
Predict which customers are likely to accept a personal loan offer.

Approach:

Explored and visualized features such as age, job, and marital status.
Trained a classifier and evaluated its accuracy.
Analyzed which customer groups are more likely to accept loans.

Results & Insights:

Middle-aged, married customers with stable jobs are more likely to accept.
Most non-acceptances were correctly predicted.
Learned how data exploration, modeling, and visualization provide actionable business insights.
